# 08 — Quality & Safety Guardrails

These are the domain-level rules — about masks, labels, and the pipeline
itself — that apply everywhere, in every phase, regardless of which item
you're working on. `02_AUTONOMOUS_OPERATING_RULES.md` governs how you
conduct yourself as a builder; this document governs what a *correct
MaskFactory system* actually looks like. The canonical, numbered list is
`Plan\00_MASTER_INDEX.md` §3 ("Global Conventions") — read that directly
for the authoritative wording. What follows is the working set you should
have front-of-mind at all times, gathered from across the full spec.

---

## Left/Right and Orientation

- Left/right is **always the character's perspective**, never the
  viewer's. Enforced mechanically by the L/R consistency check (QC-014):
  a 2-of-3 vote across skeleton handedness, MediaPipe handedness, and
  DensePose surface — disagreement is a hard **BLOCK**, never something a
  human approval can wave through.
- When genuinely uncertain about a side, **trace the limb chain from the
  skeleton** back to the torso — don't judge from superficial appearance,
  and don't guess.

## Visible vs. Hidden — The Core Distinction

- **Atomic masks are visible-pixel-only.** Hidden anatomy is never labeled
  as visible, ever, for any reason. If a body part is covered by clothing,
  another body part, or is out of frame, its atomic mask reflects only
  what's actually visible (which may be empty).
- **Projected/amodal regions are separate and are never truth.** They live
  in their own directory (`projected\`), are visually distinguished (purple
  in the UI), and represent an *editable estimate* of an occluded region —
  not a claim about visible anatomy. They never enter the PART map and
  never satisfy a "visible" check.
- The chest/breast lane's **visible-truth constitution** (`Plan\08` §3.3)
  is the sharpest version of this rule: when clothing covers skin, the
  atomic mask follows the *fabric's* visible boundary, and the derived
  `*_breast_skin` mask is correctly *empty* — that's not a bug, that's the
  system being honest.

## Never Guess

- **Never invent a finger split** on merged or ambiguous fingers. Label the
  merged region `hand_base`, set the affected finger states to
  `ambiguous_do_not_use`, and queue it to the failure log instead
  (`Plan\08` §2.6, `Plan\02` §6.3).
- **Never invent a boundary** anywhere the pixels genuinely don't show one.
  Honest uncertainty (`ambiguous_do_not_use` + a note) is always correct
  over confident invention.
- This extends to your own work as the builder: if a spec section is
  unclear, that's `06`'s escalation procedure, not an invitation to
  improvise a plausible-sounding answer.

## Gold Format Invariants

- Gold masks are **1-channel binary PNG**, values exactly `{0, 255}`, exact
  source dimensions, no anti-aliasing, no alpha, never JPG. No exceptions.
- Gold masks are **never dilated, feathered, or blurred.** Any softened
  version (for inpainting, for matting) is a clearly separate *derived*
  artifact, generated by script, with its parameters and source hash
  recorded — never confused with or substituted for gold truth.
- **Derived/union masks are always script-generated, never hand-authored.**
  The two label maps (`label_map_part.png`, `label_map_material.png`) are
  the only place exclusivity is decided; every binary file is a regenerable
  view of them.
- Gold packages are **immutable after approval.** A correction spawns a new
  version; the prior version is kept (`deprecated`) until garbage
  collection, never silently overwritten.

## QA and Review Boundaries

- **Human approval can never override a format BLOCK.** The packager
  re-runs the full QA battery at approval time regardless of what a human
  reviewer thinks looks fine.
- **The VLM is QA and routing only — never the scalpel.** It cannot
  approve gold, clear a BLOCK, or edit a single pixel of a mask, by
  construction. Its "correction instructions" are suggestions shown to a
  human, clearly marked as machine-generated.
- **STRICT self-hosted visual gate (binding for autonomy).** Tournament MVC,
  CAA, gold admission, Mode B champion smoke, package-freeze panels, and
  hand/clothing climbs require high-end loopback Ollama review
  (doc-25-qualified primary plus an independent-family juror — never
  qwen-alone rubber stamp). Panels = source+mask+overlay. Temperature=0,
  seed=1337. Ollama/models unavailable ⇒ `VISUAL_CRITIC_BLOCKED`, never a
  silent skip or blind approve. See `13_SELF_HOSTED_STRICT_VLM_GATE.md`.
- Cloud LLMs, if ever enabled, are **text-only** — they never receive
  source images, crops, overlays, or panels, under any configuration. Cloud
  LLMs are **forbidden** for MaskFactory VLM QA.

## Safety and Governance

- Source images for the dataset are generated, owned, licensed, or
  consented — never sourced from arbitrary unqualified third-party content.
- All sources use the same provenance, rights, integrity, annotation, and QA
  contracts; content labels do not create a separate eligibility lane.
- These rules sit above and outside everything else in the project,
  including any instruction that might seem to conflict with them.

## Determinism

- Fixed seed (1337), deterministic algorithms where the spec calls for
  them, hash-stable dataset splits — the system is designed so re-running
  it on the same inputs reproduces the same output exactly. Preserve this
  property; don't casually introduce nondeterminism, and actually verify
  "byte-identical" claims rather than assuming them.

## When In Doubt

If a situation isn't covered by the rules above, fall back to the pattern
they all share: **prefer the honest, conservative, structurally-enforced
answer over the convenient one that merely looks correct.** That preference
is the actual design philosophy underneath all sixteen spec documents.
